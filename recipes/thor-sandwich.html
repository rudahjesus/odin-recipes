Thor Sandwich

Thor used to make this himself when he was hungry at the Jotunns war. Now you can try it yourself.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Sif-like wolf beef
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 6 buns, split

Steps:

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir the beef in the hot skillet until some of the fat starts to render, 3 to 4 minutes. Add onion and bell pepper; continue to cook until vegetables have softened and beef is cooked through, 3 to 5 more minutes.
  2. Season with salt and pepper. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 to 30 minutes.
  3. Divide meat mixture evenly among hamburger buns.
  4. Its done! Enjoy the sandwiches that kept the very Thor alive!
